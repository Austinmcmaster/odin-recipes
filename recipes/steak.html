Steak Dinner

Description

This recipe is quite simple. We have a combination of small baked potatoes which are sautted a little after to give a nice crunchy texture. We have asparagus associated with salt and butter boiled. Then we have a beautiful ribye which is cooked to a beatiful medium rare color. Flavored with thyme, salt and pepper, butter. Wondrous meal.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Asparagus
  • Small potatoes
  • Green Onions
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Thyme & Lemon

Steps

  1. Place the ribeye in the oven and cook at 425 degrees to the desired color.
  2. Set asparagus in a pot of water. Place 1/4 stick of butter in the water and boil the asparagus until soft. Add solt and small amount of olive oil.
  3. Cut small potatoes in half. Place olive oil on the pan. Salt and pepper the potatoes. Bake at 425 degrees.
  4. Cut and dice garlic
  5. Place Olive oil in pan to keep warm. Place some green onion and garlic to cook.
  6. Once the garlic is cooked combine the 3 cooked items in the pan
  7. Salt and pepper the lot and serve.
