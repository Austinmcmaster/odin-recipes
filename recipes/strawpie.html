Strawberry Pie Recipe

Description

Strawberry pie! A glorius dish to behold. Simply Strawberries with a glaze combined with a pie crust and cooled in a fridge for an hour. Serve with whip cream and this is a fantastic dessert.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Pie crust
  • Whip Cream
  • Strawberry Glaze

Steps

  1. Remove strawberry greenery first
  2. Slice strawberries into pieces. Either sliced or diced based on preference
  3. Combine strawberry glaze with strawberries and stir
  4. Cool in the fridge for 30 minutes
  5. Place mixture in pie crust
  6. Cut and place whip cream to serve
