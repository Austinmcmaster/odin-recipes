Peanut Butter and Jelly recipe

Description

The classic peanut butter and jelly sandwitch with a few added items. Personally I love green apples, sliced which we could eat with peanut butter. I also love cool ranch doritos. There is no competition in my opinion. I forgot to mention my taste in jelly. I love that of raspberry. This is the ideal lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bread(specifically whole wheat with lots of nutrients and fiber)
  • Chunky Peanut Butter
  • Raspberries
  • Cool Ranch Doritos
  • Granny Smith Apples
  • Lemonade

Steps

  1. Place peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Place raspberry jelly on the other slice and combine
  3. Slice apples and place small amount of peanut butter on plate.
  4. Grab a bag of doritos and a lemonade and feast.
